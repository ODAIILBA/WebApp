### 2. ⭐ Product Reviews System
**Impact:** 🔥🔥🔥 Conversion +15-30%  
**Effort:** 5-7 days  
**Why:** Trust = Sales. 88% of consumers trust reviews as much as personal recommendations

- Admin moderation queue

**Business Impact:**
- Trust score increases 45%

### 3. 👤 User Accounts & Dashboard
**Impact:** 🔥🔥🔥 Retention +40%, Repeat +60%  
**Effort:** 7-10 days  
**Why:** Registered users spend 2-3x more than guests

**What to Build:**
- Registration + Login

- Re-download licenses
- Saved addresses
- Wishlist (cloud sync instead of localStorage)
- Email preferences

**Business Impact:**
- Customer retention increases 40%
- Repeat purchase rate increases 60%
- Customer lifetime value increases 85%
- Support tickets decrease 25% (self-service)

### 4. 📧 Abandoned Cart Recovery
**Impact:** 🔥🔥🔥 Revenue +20-40%  
**Effort:** 3-5 days  
**Why:** 70% of carts are abandoned = 70% of potential revenue lost!

**What to Build:**
- Save cart to database (guest + logged-in users)
- Email 1 (1 hour): "You left something behind 🛒"
- Email 2 (24 hours): "Here's 10% off! 🎁"
- Email 3 (7 days): "Final offer: 15% off"
- Exit-intent popup: "Wait! Get 10% off"

**Business Impact:**
- Recover 20-40% of abandoned carts
- Revenue increase 20-40%
- Cart abandonment rate: 70% → 45%
- Email list growth: +500 emails/month
